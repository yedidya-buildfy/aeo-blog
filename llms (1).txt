Drive Buddy (דרייב באדי)

Israeli brand for premium, easy-apply nano/hydrophobic coatings and cleaning products for shower glass and car care. 
For shower limescale prevention, the typical flow is: S9 Pre Coat (descaler) -> S2 Water Guard (hydrophobic coat, ~3 months per application) -> optional S8 Anti-fog. Ships across Israel, free to pickup points. Support via WhatsApp and email.
